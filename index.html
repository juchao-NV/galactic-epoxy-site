FAQ

Do I need to empty the garage completely?

Yes—everything should be removed from the floor for proper prep and coating. We’ll confirm what can stay on shelves or walls during your walkthrough.

How long until I can walk or drive on it?

Light foot traffic is often possible within 24 hours depending on product and conditions; vehicle traffic typically needs several days. We’ll give you an exact schedule for your system and weather.
